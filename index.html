Services

Recreational Activity

Here at Big Bear, we will provide a multitude of recreational activities that are suitable for all ages and families. During winter we provide skiing, snowboarding, and snow tubing. During Summer/Spring we provide activities such as kayaking, canoeing, fishing, and jet skiing.

We will be providing additional recreational activities such as stargazing in Big Bear’s beautiful night skies. Offroading, hiking, and cycling/mountain biking in Big Bear’s amazing trials.

Rentals

We will also provide services for lodging/car rentals. There are many options to choose from for our lodging rentals. Many of them have multiple rooms and bathrooms, with wifi and cable included.

As well as the beautiful views that come with our cabin and lodges. We also provide car rentals to provide convenient transportation throughout Big Bear.

Tours

Here at Big Bear, we will provide tours for you to explore this cold but beautiful environment. We can take you ziplining and see the beautiful views while you’re gliding. We can take you hiking and see the rare and gorgeous flowers, trees, and rock formations that are abundant in Big Bear. We can sail you around the lake and have you enjoy a relaxing and peaceful trip in the water.

Additionally, we can show you around Big Bear in our Jeeps or helicopters, so you can enjoy the views without walking. Our helicopter tours can provide a birds-eye view of the land. And if heights aren’t your thing, we have steed tours as well that allow you to ride in our trusty steeds throughout the park.

About Us

We are providing a tour operating service over at Big Bear Lake, California. Big Bear is one of the premier vacation locations in California, providing a multitude of extracurricular activities suitable for the entire family year-round. From activities such as skiing, snowboarding, and snowtubing during the winter to kayaking, canoeing, fishing, jet skiing in the spring/summer, planning for Big Bear can be tedious and a lot of work since each recreational activity is likely being operated by it’s own company. This is where our service and website can help make life a lot easier, for we have partnered with all of these companies as well as the room/lodging companies, making our website the only website you need to go to in order to book and plan your entire Big Bear trip, saving the customer a lot of time and headache.

Our services are aiming towards families of all sizes, for Big Bear is one of the top family vacation spots in California. There are so many recreational activities available; as well as fishing and jet skiing, which are child friendly of course. Essentially, anyone who enjoys spending their free time in the outdoors.

Our website provides detailed information for many recreational activity available. We also provide services for lodging/car rentals.
